Don't Look

Made: 11/3/19

Designed all code for a weekend production of a first-person horror game in 3D Unity. Structured code to implement game state changes based on forward, right, and other side vectors implemented with trigonometric math. There is a safe zone with slight grey fog, a danger zone with red fog, and then a death zone with thick black fog indicating the player has lost a life. If the player loses all 3, there is a jumpscare with camera rotation focusing on the entity. 2 person team project: myself as the developer and my friend as the artist.

Teemin' Demon Beatin' Season

Made: 11/01/19

A 2D Unity C# project. Designed main grid for core mechanic. Implemented sprite transitions for paths/enemies/attacks. Structured meetings based on project scope and sprints. The player drags lines of a path onto a grid to guide enemy demons around towers that will attack them within a certain range. The goal is to prevent any demons from surviving long enough to attack the city walls. If the player successfully kills enough demons to protect the city walls, the player wins the stage. The less damage inflicted on the walls, the more stars the player wins. The stages grow more difficult. The game ends when all the stages are cleared.

The Black Dagger

Made: 09/27/19

I was the team lead, making sure that assignments were done on time, as well as keeping the project in-scope of the timeframe alloted. The Black Dagger is fantasy themed turn-based game set in the world of Ethshar. In this game, one player takes control of an assassin who has successfully cast the black dagger spell. They then try to kill priority targets scattered in the corners of the city. However, there are four magical guards, which are controlled by the other players, that have been assigned the task of taking down the assassin. Take control of the assassin, sneaking through hidden tunnel systems trying to kill all of your targets, or take the job of the magical guards and try and stop the assassin!
